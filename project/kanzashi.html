Kanzashi Flower Tutorial

Supplies Needed:

  • 1.5 Inch Double Sided Ribbon
  • Scissors
  • Sewing Pins
  • Glue Gun
  • Thread
  • Buttons or Embellishment
  • Sewing Needles
  • Ruler
  • Lighter
  • Candle (optional but recommended)

Step 1:

Cut five pieces of ribbon, 3" in length

Step 2:

Melt raw edges of ribbon with lighter or candle to prevent fraying

Step 3:

Lay ribbon horizontal. Fold top corner down to the middle and pin. Repeat with other courner

Step 4:

Fold ribbon again using the bottom corner up to the middle and pin. Repeat with other courner

Step 5:

Thread needle and sew along bottom edge, starting on the right outside corner, using a weaving stitch. Continue acrros the folded ribbon, matching stitch placement. Pull tight and tie off thread

Step 6:

Repeat with all petals. The same thread can be used for all petals. Once all petals are threaded, tie last petal to first petal to make flower

Step 7

To create pointed petals, pinch top of petal and melt the back edges together with a lighter or candel

Step 8:

Cut a round piece of felt and using the hot glue gun, attach felt to the back of flower to cover edges

Step 9:

Using a hot glue gun, glue button or embellishment on the center of the flower to finish
